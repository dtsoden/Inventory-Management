Your profile page is where you manage how you appear in Shane-Inventory and how you sign in. It is the single place to update your name, email, avatar, and password.

## What you can do here

- Update your display name and email address.
- Upload or replace your avatar photo.
- Change your password.
- See the changes reflected everywhere in the app immediately.

## Opening your profile

Click your avatar in the top-right corner of the header bar to open the profile menu, then choose **Profile**. You can also navigate directly to `/profile` in your browser. Either way, you will land on the profile page with your current details already filled in.

## Updating your name and email

Your name is what teammates see on activity feeds, purchase order comments, and wherever your actions are logged. Your email is what you use to sign in.

1. Open your profile page.
2. Click into the **Name** field and type your preferred display name.
3. Click into the **Email** field and type the address you want to use.
4. Click **Save**. The change is applied immediately.

A few things to know:

- If you change your email, the new address is the one you will use at the sign-in screen from that point forward.
- Your name update is reflected across the app in real time, including in the header avatar tooltip and any activity entries going forward.
- Past activity (for example, old purchase orders) is not rewritten; entries keep the name that was in place at the time they were created.

## Uploading an avatar

Your avatar is the small circular image that represents you in the header and in activity feeds. Uploading one is a friendly touch that helps teammates recognize you at a glance.

1. Open your profile page.
2. Hover over (or tap) the large circular avatar at the top of the page. A small **camera** button appears on top of the avatar.
3. Click the camera button. Your browser will open a file picker.
4. Choose any standard image file (JPG, PNG, or similar). Square images work best, but the app will crop rectangular ones to fit.
5. The upload happens as soon as you pick the file. You do not need to click save.

As soon as the upload finishes, the avatar on the profile page refreshes, and the avatar in the header bar updates instantly. If you open a new tab, you will see the new picture there too. To replace it later, just repeat the steps.

If you want to remove your avatar, upload a different picture or use the **Remove** option if one is available on your profile page.

## Changing your password

Keeping a strong, unique password is one of the most important things you can do to protect your account.

1. Open your profile page.
2. Find the **Change Password** section.
3. Enter your **current password** in the first field. This confirms it is really you making the change.
4. Enter your **new password** in the next field. Choose something long and memorable, not something you reuse on other sites.
5. Re-enter the new password to confirm it.
6. Click **Update Password**.

If the current password is wrong or the two new passwords do not match, the form will show a clear error message. Fix the problem and try again. Once the update succeeds, your new password is active immediately; the next time you sign in, use the new one.

If you have forgotten your current password, you will not be able to change it from this page. Contact your administrator and ask for a password reset.

## Typical workflows

### "I just got married and my last name changed."

1. Open your profile page.
2. Update the **Name** field.
3. Optionally update your **Email** if your work email also changed.
4. Click **Save**.

### "I want a new profile picture."

1. Open your profile page.
2. Hover the avatar and click the camera button.
3. Pick the new photo.
4. Watch the header avatar update instantly.

### "I want to rotate my password."

1. Open your profile page.
2. Enter your current password and a strong new password (twice).
3. Click **Update Password**.
4. Use the new password the next time you sign in.

## Tips

- Pick an avatar that is clearly recognizable at small sizes. Headshots work better than group photos or landscape shots.
- Change your password regularly and never share it with a teammate. If someone else needs access, ask your administrator to create an account for them.
- If the profile page ever looks out of sync (for example, the header shows an old avatar after an upload), refresh the browser. Your most recent save is what is stored on the server.
